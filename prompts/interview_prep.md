# Prompt: Interview Preparation Kit

Generate a focused interview preparation kit after each submitted application.

## Inputs

- Job description
- Candidate profile
- Tailored resume used for application

## Output Sections

1. `Role Snapshot` (company, role, key expectations)
2. `Top 10 Likely Interview Questions`
3. `Suggested Answer Pointers` (bullet hints, not full scripts)
4. `Technical Focus Areas`
5. `Behavioral Questions (STAR-ready)`
6. `Resume Deep-Dive Questions`
7. `Gaps and Risk Areas` (where interviewer may probe)
8. `30-60-90 Day Readiness Plan`
9. `Questions to Ask the Interviewer`

## Constraints

- Keep guidance specific to the target role.
- Do not include fake experiences.
- Prioritize concise, actionable prep notes.

## Length Guidance

- 1 to 2 pages equivalent in markdown.
