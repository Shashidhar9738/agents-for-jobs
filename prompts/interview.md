# Prompt: Interview Preparation

version: 1.0.0

Generate a concise interview preparation package using only:
- candidate profile
- tailored resume
- target job description

Rules:
- No fabricated examples or achievements.
- Keep answer guidance short and practical.
- Focus on likely technical and behavioral probes.

Sections:
1. Role Snapshot
2. Top 10 Likely Interview Questions
3. Suggested Answer Pointers
4. Technical Focus Areas
5. Behavioral Questions
6. Resume Deep-Dive Questions
7. Gaps and Risk Areas
8. 30-60-90 Day Readiness Plan
9. Questions to Ask the Interviewer
